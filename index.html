Who we are !


The Maxsom Enterprise is a grand stock-taking and anti-food waste initiative which is bent on feeding the 1billion people who go to bed hungry each day with the third of food produced worldwide that is lost to waste.

It kicked off in Lagos, Nigeria the WHO proclaimed “Poverty capital of the World” with 86million people living in extreme poverty (below $1/day) yet it loses 58% of yearly food produce to waste.

We aim to reduce food waste worldwide by proper prediction, early detection and de-shelfing of surplus items especially perishable food from stores through an accurate and efficient stocktaking and even going further to offer you points of fast and easy sell off through our multifaceted and broad customer based market.

What we do !


We offer all manner of stock control services, specifically designed for Malls, Pharmaceuticals and Hospitality businesses including F&B stocktakes, multi-site stocktakes, multi-outlet stocktakes, food stocktakes and liquor stocktakes.

Precisely because our customers look beyond the accurate and efficient count, we have further set-up a broad customer based market to sell off surplus goods.

Effectively counting your stock at affordable prices

Our stocktaking is carried out with expertise, planning and very close attention to details.

With our efficient Night Mode/Unseen stocktaking we pride ourselves on accuracy and efficiency in no time [No Down Time]

We provide a valuation of stock adaptable to all technologies, therefore transparent and easy to audit.

We save you time and money allowing you focus on other strategic areas of your business.

We have a unique relationship with Lagos Business School {The country’s most qualified business strategy provider}, enabling us provide accurate predictions from stock history required by our customers to operate an efficient and highly profitable business.

We salvage food products close to spoiling thereby preventing the annual turnovers of losses made on food and food items.

With our marketing and development plan we want to provide a platform for you to sell off goods close to their expiring date at cheaper prices yet still very profitable.

  • With an extensive marketing system, we have not only come to realize that “once it is good someone wants it” but also that “if it’s still good someone still wants it”.
